# App Store Connect Submission Guide

## App Description

Best Day Ministries empowers individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities through faith, community, and meaningful employment opportunities.

**Our Mission:**
We believe every person is created with purpose and value. Through inclusive programs, community events, and the Joy House Store marketplace, we spread JOY, hope, and purpose to individuals and families in the special needs community.

**Features:**
• Browse and attend community events
• Connect with other families and supporters
• Shop handmade goods from Joy House Store
• Support meaningful employment for individuals with disabilities
• Sponsor a "Bestie" and make a lasting impact
• Access devotionals, discussions, and faith resources

**About Our Organizations:**
- **Best Day Ministries** (508(c)(1)(A) religious organization) - Receives donations and sponsorships to fund programs and support individuals with disabilities
- **Joy House Store** (501(c)(3) nonprofit) - Marketplace for handmade goods that provides meaningful employment

## App Review Notes

**Payment Processing Explanation:**

This app uses Stripe for payment processing in two scenarios, both of which are exempt from In-App Purchase requirements:

1. **Physical Goods Sales (Joy House Store)**
   - Our marketplace sells physical, handmade products created by individuals with disabilities
   - Per Apple Guideline 3.1.3(e): "Goods and services... to be consumed outside of the app" are exempt from IAP
   - Joy House Store is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: [YOUR 501c3 EIN])

2. **Charitable Donations & Sponsorships**
   - Donations and "Bestie" sponsorships support Best Day Ministries programs
   - Per Apple Guideline 3.2.1(vi): Approved nonprofits may collect donations via external payment
   - Best Day Ministries is a registered 508(c)(1)(A) religious organization, which the IRS recognizes as equivalent to 501(c)(3) for tax-exempt purposes

**No digital goods or services are sold in this app.** All payments are for physical merchandise or charitable contributions to registered tax-exempt organizations.
